How does a car get data?

Lidar is a surveying method that measures distance to a target by illuminating the target with pulsed laser light and measuring the reflected pulses with a sensor. Differences in laser return times and wavelengths can then be used to make digital 3-D representationsof the target.

How does the Car predict data?

The car interpretes the collected data with a neural network. This is is a class of deep, feed-forward artificial neural networks, most commonly applied to analyzing visual imagery.
